# Utility calculator conventions and sources

This catalogue deliberately shows its rule before its technical evidence. Ganak's
Vedic calculators use Lahiri ayanamsa and mean Rahu/Ketu. Western calculators use
the tropical zodiac and are labelled separately.

## Rule map

| Calculator | Implemented convention | Reference basis |
|---|---|---|
| Rashi, Sun sign, Lagna, Nakshatra | Sidereal longitude after Lahiri ayanamsa; 27 equal nakshatras; exact local birth time and coordinates | Government of India, *Report of the Calendar Reform Committee* (Lahiri/Chitrapaksha basis); B. V. Raman, *Hindu Predictive Astrology* |
| Baby-name initials | One traditional sound for each of four padas in each of 27 nakshatras. Ganak uses Drik Panchang's paired Latin display and Devanagari akshara at the same pada index; it does not mix alternative aliases from other tables. Family-language spelling may still vary after the recommended starting sound. | [Drik Panchang English 108-pada naming table](https://www.drikpanchang.com/swar-siddhanta/nakshatra/nakshatra-pada-swar-siddhanta.html) and its parallel [Hindi table](https://www.drikpanchang.com/swar-siddhanta/nakshatra/nakshatra-pada-swar-siddhanta.html?lang=hi), verified 2026-07-24 |
| Mangal Dosha | Mars in 1, 2, 4, 7, 8 or 12, independently from Lagna, Moon and Venus | Common three-reference Jyotisha practice; because cancellation rules vary by lineage, own/exalted dignity is shown as mitigation and never silently cancels the result |
| Kala Sarpa | All seven classical planets strictly within either Rahu–Ketu semicircle; nodes excluded | Explicit geometric convention; no deterministic harm claim and no invented subtype when boundary criteria fail |
| Sade Sati | Sidereal Saturn in the sign before, same sign as, or sign after the natal Moon | Standard gochara definition; three phases are labelled rising, middle and setting |
| Shraddha Tithi | Tithi and paksha at the recorded instant; annual observance follows corresponding tithi with aparahna precedence | *Narada Purana*, chapter 28 (aparahna/tithi selection); IGNCA, *The Indian Calendar* (annual tithi of death) |
| Pancha Pakshi | Five nakshatra groups map to Vulture, Owl, Crow, Cock and Peacock in Shukla Paksha; order reverses in Krishna Paksha | Published Pancha Pakshi bird tables. Lineage-specific sub-yama activity schedules are not synthesized without an agreed table |
| Western natal | Tropical zodiac; Sun, Moon and ascendant; conjunction 8°, opposition 8°, trine/square 6°, sextile 5° | Standard modern Western natal convention; aspect orbs are stated in product copy |
| Western relationship | Cross-chart tropical major aspects; shortest-arc midpoint composite | Standard synastry and midpoint-composite conventions; no compatibility verdict |

## Product boundaries

These are intentionally absent from the approved catalogue: numerology
(Mulank/Bhagyank/Namank), birthstone, Chinese zodiac/calendar, Western
transit/progression, Vastu, Feng Shui, gemstone and Rudraksha calculators.

The Pancha Pakshi result is a birth-bird calculator, not a fabricated precision
daily sub-yama timetable. Published traditions differ in their finer activity
tables; expanding it requires selecting and naming a specific lineage table.
