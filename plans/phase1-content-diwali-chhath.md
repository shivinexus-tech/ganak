# Phase 1 content slice — Diwali cluster and Chhath

Status: implemented for review on 2026-07-19. Location anchor: New Delhi, India,
2026. This note records the source and deciding day-part behind every new calendar
entry; it is not user-facing ritual copy.

## What was added

| Key | New Delhi 2026 | Deciding rule | User-facing scope |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Govatsa Dwadashi / Vasu Baras | 5 Nov | Kartik Krishna Dwadashi during Pradosh | Bilingual name, short gloss and timing only |
| Dhanteras / Dhantrayodashi | 6 Nov | Trayodashi during Pradosh | Bilingual name, short gloss and timing only |
| Kali Chaudas | 7 Nov | Chaturdashi during Nishita | Explicitly marked regional; no invented vidhi |
| Narak Chaturdashi / Chhoti Diwali | 8 Nov | Chaturdashi during Arunodaya before sunrise | Bilingual name, short gloss and timing only |
| Govardhan Puja / Annakut | 10 Nov | Kartik Shukla Pratipada during Pratahkala | Bilingual name, short gloss and timing only |
| Bhai Dooj | 11 Nov | Kartik Shukla Dwitiya during Aparahna | Bilingual name, short gloss and timing only |
| Chhath day 1 — Nahay Khay | 13 Nov | Two civil days before Shashthi sunset | Four-day sequence label |
| Chhath day 2 — Kharna | 14 Nov | One civil day before Shashthi sunset | Four-day sequence label |
| Chhath day 3 — Sandhya Arghya | 15 Nov | Kartik Shukla Shashthi at sunset | Existing sourced full vidhi is attached here |
| Chhath day 4 — Usha Arghya and Paran | 16 Nov | First sunrise after Shashthi sunset | Four-day sequence label |

The existing Diwali entry remains 8 Nov (Amavasya during Pradosh). Chhath is
deliberately shown as four connected days so the product does not misrepresent the
Shashthi sunset as the entire observance.

## Content boundary

No new mantra, sankalpa or detailed Diwali-cluster vidhi was written in this slice.
Those entries expose only sourced identity, sequence and deciding time. The Chhath
card reuses the bilingual vidhi already sourced in `plans/vrat-vidhis.md` from
Drik and Bihar Tourism. Regional practices remain labelled as regional rather than
being blended into a false universal rule.
