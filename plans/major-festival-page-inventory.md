# Major festival substantive-page inventory

Status: active launch audit under `CODEX-P0-MAJOR-FESTIVAL-COVERAGE-01`.

## Standard

A reviewed substantive page must be bilingual and answer-first, then cover:
significance; a safe household worship path; food/fasting boundaries; stories
with their scope labelled; regional variation; completion/udyapan where relevant;
and safety without rewriting the religious practice. A permanent route and a
metadata paragraph alone do not count.
