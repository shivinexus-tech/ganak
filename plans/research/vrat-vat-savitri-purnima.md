# Vat Savitri / Vat Purnima — Research Draft

**Status:** DRAFT — OWNER REVIEW REQUIRED  
**Research date:** 2026-07-20  
**Scope:** The Amavasya and Purnima regional forms, with one shared story and clearly selected calendar tradition. No app code has been changed.

## 1. The product decision this guide must enforce

Ganak must **not** silently choose one date for everyone:

- **Vat Savitri on Jyeshtha Amavasya** is the common Purnimanta/North Indian form.
- **Vat Purnima on Jyeshtha Purnima** is the Amanta form followed especially in Maharashtra, Gujarat and parts of southern/western India.

The two dates are about fifteen days apart, but the Savitri–Satyavan narrative and central banyan-tree worship are shared. Drik Panchang explicitly documents this distinction. [Drik Panchang — Vat Purnima](https://www.drikpanchang.com/festivals/savitri/vat-purnima-date-time.html)

The user should select a **family/region tradition** once; Ganak may recommend based on region but must show and explain the other date. Location alone is not permission to override a family's inherited practice.

## 2. Identity and purpose

The vrata remembers Savitri's steadfastness and wisdom in winning back Satyavan's life from Yama. Married women traditionally observe it for their husband's well-being and longevity. The banyan tree is central to the worship. [Maharashtra Tourism](https://maharashtratourism.gov.in/festivals/vat-purnima/), [Drik Panchang](https://www.drikpanchang.com/festivals/savitri/vat-purnima-date-time.html)

Ganak should describe the traditional married-woman observance faithfully. It may welcome anyone to read the story or offer prayer, but it must not rewrite the vrata's established identity in order to modernize it.

## 3. Verified common practice

- Bathe, wear clean/traditional clothes, keep the vowed fast, and worship at a banyan tree. [Maharashtra Tourism](https://maharashtratourism.gov.in/festivals/vat-purnima/)
- Offer water, flowers and commonly used auspicious materials; tie thread around the banyan while making pradakshina. [Maharashtra Tourism](https://maharashtratourism.gov.in/festivals/vat-purnima/), [Hindu Community and Cultural Center, Livermore](https://livermoretemple.org/cms/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Vat-Savitrhi-Vrat.pdf)
- Hear or read the Savitri–Satyavan katha and pray for the husband's health, longevity and the well-being of married life. [Maharashtra Tourism](https://maharashtratourism.gov.in/festivals/vat-purnima/), [Utsav — Ministry of Tourism](https://utsav.gov.in/view-event/vat-pournima-konkani-mahila-mahotsav-1)
- A detailed shodashopachara form exists, but its mantras and mudras should not be presented as compulsory for a beginner. Ganak can link to a priest-led/full ritual while keeping the household card simple. [Drik Panchang — puja vidhi](https://www.drikpanchang.com/festivals/savitri/puja-vidhi/vat-savitri-puja-vidhi.html)

## 5. Fasting, completion and udyapan

Maharashtra Tourism describes a strict fast, but the reviewed government page does not define one universal food list or exact breaking time. [Maharashtra Tourism](https://maharashtratourism.gov.in/festivals/vat-purnima/)

One older general Hindu resource describes a multi-day form and breaking the fast on the following day, while contemporary regional practice pages often describe a one-day fast. This is a real practice difference and must not be blended into one rule. [Hindu Universe](https://www.hindunet.org/festivals/vat_savitri_vrat.htm)

Therefore:

- Ganak should state **strict fast** as the traditional form where the selected tradition says so.
- Food/water details and completion should follow the selected family or temple rule.
- Ganak should not show one universal paran clock until it has a tradition-specific sourced rule.
- No universal udyapan should be invented for every observer. Counted or specially undertaken vows require family/priest guidance.

## 6. Research gaps — do not guess

4. A sourced diaspora substitute when no living banyan or temple worship is available. Ganak should not tell a user to cut a branch or invent a symbolic replacement.

## 8. Proposed English user-facing card

### Vat Savitri / Vat Purnima

**Your selected tradition**

This page is showing **[Vat Savitri on Jyeshtha Amavasya / Vat Purnima on Jyeshtha Purnima]** because you selected **[tradition]**. The other regional observance is on **[date]**. Change tradition if your family follows that date.

**What to do today**

Observe your family's fast, worship Savitri and the banyan tree, tie the sacred thread while making pradakshina, and read or hear the Savitri–Satyavan story. Pray for your husband's well-being and longevity and for steadiness and dharma in married life.

**Simple worship**

1. Bathe and prepare the customary offerings used in your family.
2. At a banyan tree, offer water and flowers and worship Savitri–Satyavan according to family custom.
3. Tie the thread around the tree while making the customary pradakshina. Do not state a fixed number unless your tradition specifies it.
4. Read or hear the Savitri–Satyavan katha.
5. Offer prayer and complete the fast according to your family's Amavasya or Purnima tradition.

If no suitable banyan is accessible, join a temple observance or ask your family priest/temple for its accepted substitute. Ganak does not advise cutting a living branch.

**Plain-language sankalpa**

“On this [Vat Savitri/Vat Purnima], I remember Savitri's steadfast dharma and observe my family's vrata for my husband's well-being, longevity and a righteous married life.”

This is a plain-language intention, not a prescribed Sanskrit mantra.

**Fast and completion**

The traditional observance includes a strict fast. Follow the exact food, water and completion rule of your family or temple. Regional sources do not establish one universal paran clock for both Amavasya and Purnima forms, so Ganak should not merge them into a made-up time.

**Udyapan**

No single universal udyapan was verified. Complete a counted or specially undertaken vow with the family priest, guru or temple that gave its rules.

**Health note — separate from the religious rule**

This note does not change the traditional strict fast. If strict fasting may be medically unsafe—especially with pregnancy, diabetes, kidney illness, an eating disorder, frailty or medicines—seek medical advice before taking the vow. Do not stop or retime prescribed medicine without professional guidance.

## 9. Proposed Hindi user-facing card

### वट सावित्री / वट पूर्णिमा

**आपकी चुनी हुई परंपरा**

यह पृष्ठ **[ज्येष्ठ अमावस्या की वट सावित्री / ज्येष्ठ पूर्णिमा की वट पूर्णिमा]** दिखा रहा है, क्योंकि आपने **[परंपरा]** चुनी है। दूसरा क्षेत्रीय व्रत **[तारीख]** को है। यदि आपके परिवार में वह तिथि मानी जाती है तो परंपरा बदलें।

**आज क्या करें**

अपने परिवार के नियम के अनुसार व्रत रखें, सावित्री और वट-वृक्ष की पूजा करें, प्रदक्षिणा करते हुए पवित्र धागा बाँधें, और सावित्री–सत्यवान की कथा पढ़ें या सुनें। पति के स्वास्थ्य और दीर्घायु तथा दाम्पत्य में धैर्य और धर्म की प्रार्थना करें।

**सरल पूजा**

1. स्नान करके परिवार में प्रचलित पूजन-सामग्री तैयार करें।
2. वट-वृक्ष के पास जल और फूल अर्पित करके पारिवारिक रीति से सावित्री–सत्यवान का पूजन करें।
3. वट-वृक्ष की प्रदक्षिणा करते हुए धागा बाँधें। जब तक आपकी परंपरा संख्या न बताए, गणक कोई निश्चित संख्या नहीं बताएगा।
4. सावित्री–सत्यवान की कथा पढ़ें या सुनें।
5. अपनी अमावस्या या पूर्णिमा परंपरा के अनुसार प्रार्थना और व्रत पूरा करें।

यदि वट-वृक्ष उपलब्ध न हो तो मंदिर के सामूहिक पूजन में सम्मिलित हों या परिवार-पुरोहित/मंदिर से मान्य विकल्प पूछें। गणक जीवित शाखा काटने की सलाह नहीं देता।

**सरल संकल्प**

“इस [वट सावित्री/वट पूर्णिमा] पर मैं सावित्री के अडिग धर्म को स्मरण करते हुए अपने पति के स्वास्थ्य, दीर्घायु और धर्ममय दाम्पत्य के लिए अपने परिवार में प्रचलित व्रत रखती हूँ।”

यह सरल भाव-संकल्प है, निर्धारित संस्कृत मंत्र नहीं।

**उपवास और समापन**

पारंपरिक अनुष्ठान में कठोर उपवास शामिल है। भोजन, जल और व्रत पूरा करने का ठीक वही नियम रखें जो आपके परिवार या मंदिर में मान्य है। अमावस्या और पूर्णिमा—दोनों के लिए एक ही सार्वभौमिक पारण-समय प्रमाणित नहीं है; गणक उन्हें मिलाकर अनुमानित समय नहीं दिखाएगा।

**उद्यापन**

एक सार्वभौमिक उद्यापन प्रमाणित नहीं हुआ। निश्चित संख्या या विशेष संकल्प का व्रत उसी परिवार-पुरोहित, गुरु या मंदिर के मार्गदर्शन में पूरा करें जहाँ से उसके नियम मिले हैं।

**स्वास्थ्य-सूचना — धार्मिक नियम से अलग**

यह सूचना पारंपरिक कठोर उपवास को नहीं बदलती। गर्भावस्था, मधुमेह, गुर्दे की बीमारी, भोजन-संबंधी विकार, अधिक कमजोरी या नियमित दवा की स्थिति में व्रत लेने से पहले चिकित्सकीय सलाह लें। चिकित्सकीय मार्गदर्शन के बिना निर्धारित दवा बंद न करें और उसका समय न बदलें।
