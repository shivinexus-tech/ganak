## Timing kind

`moonrise`

## Ritual essentials

1. Vrat sunrise → moonrise; nirjala common; sargi optional (Punjab etc.).
2. Evening: Gauri/Parvati, Shiva parivar, katha, thali.
3. Moon darshan (sieve/cloth per custom), arghya, water from husband, parana.
4. Sankalpa: पति की दीर्घायु, सौभाग्य — not therapy English.
